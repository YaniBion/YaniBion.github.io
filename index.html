Research

I joined Particle Astrophysics research group in February 2018 where I started working with my advisor Prof. Laura Baudis. I'm interested in rare events physics such as interactions given by dark matter candidates as axions and WIMPs, the nature of neutrinos (Majorana or Diract particles). I work with data analysis, MC simulations, statistical inference and in the development of new time projection chambers with LXe.
